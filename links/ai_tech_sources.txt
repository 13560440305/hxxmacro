1. NewsAPI (technology) - 需要正式API密钥，demo仅返回提示

   API: https://hacker-news.firebaseio.com/v0/topstories.json?print=pretty

4. The Verge AI板块

5. arXiv API (学术论文)
   API: http://export.arxiv.org/api/query?search_query=cat:cs.AI&max_results=10
